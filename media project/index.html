About Seinfeld

Seinfeld is a sitcom that ran from 1989 to 1998. It was created by Larry David and Jerry Seinfeld and set predominantly in an apartment building in Manhattan's Upper West Side in New York City. "The show about nothing", revolves around the mundane lives of Jerry and his friends, Elaine, Kramer, and George.
